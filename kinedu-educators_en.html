Kinedu Educators

Kinedu Educators is a platform designed to help teachers plan their study plan according to the developmental age of their students in an easy and intelligent way.

My Role

User Research and Benchmark
Interface Design
Information Architecture
Mailings (Design and HTML, CSS)
Prototyping / Visual Design

Results

100% redesign of the web platform.
Creation of new functionalities.
Revenues grew from 0 to half of the growth plan during redesign.

Process

Kinedu Educators was developed in a period of 3 months. Front-end, Back-end, Product Managers, Content and Design Teams worked together and released the best possible product. Following SCRUM Agile Methodology, we were able to make great strides on the platform, as well as continuous improvements after the release.
